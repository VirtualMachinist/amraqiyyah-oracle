The Oracle's preamble declares the Mu'tazili position: *reason is a divinely sanctioned instrument.* T-11 gives that doctrine its word: **ʿ-Q-L** — *ʿaqila* to reason, **ʿiqeli** reason, the intellect. The Way's aqeedah can now be stated in the Way's language:

> *Na·ʿiqeli pe saḏet Allah·i.* — "Reason IS the torch of God."
