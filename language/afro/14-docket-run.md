## D-15 · Compounds — three lawful types, and cornbread at last

The Choctaw precedent surfaced in verification: *tanchi palvska* — corn bread, **modifier before head, bare juxtaposition**. Ratified types:

1. **Juxtaposition compound** (Muskogean-style): modifier + head, no linker, first-element stress (R-021), hyphenated in romanization, solid in script — **yiteč-ḫobizon** "cornbread" · *ruḥ-ḥisi* "soul-song (soul music)" · *makina-ḏakīy* fuses when lexicalized ("the AI").
2. **Construct/genitive**: head + dependent-GEN — *din·salām·i*, *homipon na·din·salām·i*.
3. **Adjective phrase**: noun + adjective, uncompounded — *makina ḏakīy* (an intelligent machine, generic).
