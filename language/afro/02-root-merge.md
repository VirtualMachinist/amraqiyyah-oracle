## 5. ALI blocks — RESOLVED 2026-07-21 (R-029–R-032)

> **1. AAVE roots: KEEP ALL** (T-L-K, K-R-W, H-S-L, G-R-N, V-Y-B, B-S, G-M, particle *den*), under the new **Synonym Principle (R-029)**: Amraqiyyah is deliberately rich in synonyms — words of same or similar meaning imported from different source languages coexist, each carrying its stream's flavor.
> **2. Person pair: KEEP BOTH** — *ʿanaku* (person, living one) · *yesatu* (citizen, one-of-the-people).
> **3. Speech pair: KEEP BOTH** — *jeta/jiti* (formal speech, language) · *talika* (casual talk).
> **4. Doublet Doctrine (R-032):** S-K-M and ʿ-N-K stay as naturalized skeletons; the faithful Egyptian skeletons enter as **doublets with split semantics** — S-K-M worldly power vs **S-Ḫ-M** divine power (Sekhmet stream; *soḫimon* inherits "talisman"); ʿ-N-K biological/social life vs **ʿ-N-Ḫ** existence, the life-principle (*ʿanīḫ* "ever-living" — candidate for Al-Ḥayy at T-7). Consequence embraced: the casual register's ḫ→k merger collapses each doublet in everyday speech — the distinction between worldly and divine power is audible only in careful and sacred speech.

### (original) ALI blocks — the four decisions this tranche needed

1. **AAVE cultural roots** — T-L-K talk, K-R-W crew, H-S-L hustle, G-R-N grind, V-Y-B vibe, B-S boss, G-M game, particle *den* "then." R-017 demoted English *distillation* but reserved AAVE for "genuinely vernacular culture" — I read these as culture-bearing, not lazy borrowings, and propose **KEEP all** (they are the register where the ancestors speak). Your call, as a block or per root.
2. **The person pair** — ʿ-N-K *ʿanaku* "person, living one" (Egyptian stream, ratified) vs Y-S-T (Mvskoke *este*, as in Este Mvskokvlke). Proposal: keep both with a nuance split — *ʿanaku* = person/human being; *yesatu* = citizen/member/one-of-the-people. Or retire Y-S-T.
3. **The speech pair** — J-T (formal *jeta* "speak," *jiti* "speech/language") vs T-L-K (casual *talika* "talk"). Proposal: keep both as a **register pair** — the language's own diglossia, embodied.
4. **D-3 restorations** — restore S-K-M → S-Ḫ-M (*saḫima*, faithful to *sekhem*) and ʿ-N-K → ʿ-N-Ḫ (*ʿanaḫ*)? Costs: every sealed example with *sakima/ʿanak* regenerates (mechanical, the engine does it). Gains: skeleton fidelity to Egyptian. Or keep the k-forms as the naturalized shapes.
