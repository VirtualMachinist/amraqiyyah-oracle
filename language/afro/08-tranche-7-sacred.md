## 3. The Two Eternities

Egyptian holds time in two hands, and now Amraqiyyah does: **neḥeḥ** — cyclical eternity, the returning forever (sunrises, seasons, **the Great Year of the Calendar runs in neḥeḥ**) — and **ḏet** — linear eternity, the enduring forever (stone, the unchanging). *Neḥeḥ* is rootless-sacred (kin to *ḥeḥ* infinity); *ḏet* stands beside *ḏed* stability as close cousins, as in Egyptian.

> *Na·ẓilemi bilā ḏet.* — "Oppression is without eternity."
