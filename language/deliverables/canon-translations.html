TIER II-B — THE CONFUCIAN-TAOIST FOUNDATION

Tao Te Ching — Chapter 1

Jasima al·Hinā na·Tap·i
Source: Classical Chinese (received Wang Bi text) — authoritative in ambiguity (Canon rule 5)
01道可道，非常道。
Al·hina nin·ta i·qadira jata·to, la pe al·hina dahīr.
Al-hina nin-ta i-qadira jata-to, la pe al-hina dahiir. ⲁⲗϩⲓⲛⲁ ⲛⲓⲛⲧⲁ ⲓⲕ̅ⲁⲇⲓⲣⲁ ϫⲁⲧⲁⲧⲟ, ⲗⲁ ⲡⲉ ⲁⲗϩⲓⲛⲁ ⲇⲁϩⲓ̄ⲣ. الهِنَ نِنتَ اِقَدِرَ جَتَتۆ، لَ پێ الهِنَ دَهير.

The Way that one can speak is not THE substrate-eternal Way.

al· — the sacred THE for the Way itself (Ali's correction: this Way is never merely worldly); dahīr — 常 as the substrate-eternity (R-069): the Tao that contains time, as Laozi's ch.25 'standing alone, unchanging' demands; ḏet re-ruled to geometric infinity and withdrawn here. R-072: 可 restored — i·qadira + bare-stem complement (the ability auxiliary, AAVE 'can speak'), resumptive on the content verb; and the Mawangdui scrolls vindicate dahīr — the original text read 恆 heng 'enduring' (道可道也非恆道也), changed to 常 only by Emperor Wen's naming taboo: Laozi's own word was closer to dahr than the received text let on

02名可名，非常名。
Al·rini nin·ta i·qadira rana·to, la pe al·rini dahīr.
Al-rini nin-ta i-qadira rana-to, la pe al-rini dahiir. ⲁⲗⲣⲓⲛⲓ ⲛⲓⲛⲧⲁ ⲓⲕ̅ⲁⲇⲓⲣⲁ ⲣⲁⲛⲁⲧⲟ, ⲗⲁ ⲡⲉ ⲁⲗⲣⲓⲛⲓ ⲇⲁϩⲓ̄ⲣ. الرِنِ نِنتَ اِقَدِرَ رَنَتۆ، لَ پێ الرِنِ دَهير.

The name that one can name is not THE substrate-eternal name.

al·rini in parallel — the eternal Name wears the sacred article as the Way does; R-072: 可 restored in parallel with line 1 (i·qadira + bare stem)

03無名天地之始；
Al·bilā-rini pe na·tap na·pet ma na·yak·i.
Al-bilaa-rini pe na-tap na-pet ma na-yak-i. ⲁⲗⲃⲓⲗⲁ̄-ⲣⲓⲛⲓ ⲡⲉ ⲛⲁⲧⲁⲡ ⲛⲁⲡⲉⲧ ⲙⲁ ⲛⲁⲓ̈ⲁⲕⲓ. البِلا-رِنِ پێ نَتَپ نَپێت مَ نَيَكِ.

THE Nameless is the origin of heaven and earth.

bilā-compound (R-068): bilā-rini 'the Without-Name'; tap = head→origin, the Egyptian semantics of tp restored. ERRATUM R-071 (Ali's catch): the article was missing — the Nameless is an absolute, and absolutes wear the sacred THE (b is a moon letter: al· unassimilated)

04有名萬物之母。
Al·het-rini pe iška na·pa·šeyāt·i.
Al-het-rini pe ishka na-pa-sheyaat-i. ⲁⲗϩⲉⲧ-ⲣⲓⲛⲓ ⲡⲉ ⲓϣⲕⲁ ⲛⲁⲡⲁϣⲉⲓ̈ⲁ̄ⲧⲓ. الهێت-رِنِ پێ اِشكَ نَپَشێياتِ.

THE Named is the mother of the myriad things.

het-rini 'the With-Name'; 萬物 as na·pa·šeyāt, the myriad things; iška — the mother-word, inalienable everywhere else, standing bare here as pure role (R-033's citation use). ERRATUM R-071: al· added in parallel with line 3 — the Named is the same absolute seen from the manifest side (h is a moon letter: al· unassimilated)

05故常無欲，以觀其妙；
Ra·pen: en dahr bilā šiweqī, i·hariwa na·biṭenya.
Ra-pen: en dahr bilaa shiweqii, i-hariwa na-bitenya. ⲣⲁⲡⲉⲛ: ⲉⲛ ⲇⲁϩⲣ ⲃⲓⲗⲁ̄ ϣⲓⲩⲉⲕ̅ⲓ̄, ⲓϩⲁⲣⲓⲩⲁ ⲛⲁⲃⲓⲧ̣ⲉⲛⲓ̈ⲁ. رَپێن: ئێن دَهر بِلا شِوێقي، اِهَرِوَ نَبِطێنيَ.

Therefore: ever without desire, one beholds the hidden.

šiweqi — the diaspora longing serving for 欲; biṭeni = the batin (~Al-Bāṭin, Threshold Name, Hex 26). ERRATA R-071: bilā governs the genitive (šiweqī, i+i→ī — as line 9's en·ribesī always knew), and the dropped 常 returns as en dahr 'in the aeon, ever' (R-069's own root)

06常有欲，以觀其徼。
En dahr het šiweqī, i·hariwa na·ẓiherya.
En dahr het shiweqii, i-hariwa na-ziherya. ⲉⲛ ⲇⲁϩⲣ ϩⲉⲧ ϣⲓⲩⲉⲕ̅ⲓ̄, ⲓϩⲁⲣⲓⲩⲁ ⲛⲁⲍ̣ⲓϩⲉⲣⲓ̈ⲁ. ئێن دَهر هێت شِوێقي، اِهَرِوَ نَظِهێريَ.

Ever with desire, one beholds the manifest.

ẓiheri = the zahir (~Az-Zāhir, Hex 33) — Laozi's hidden-and-manifest IS the Way's own Threshold pair. ERRATA R-071: het governs the genitive (šiweqī); 常 restored as en dahr, parallel to line 5

07此兩者，同出而異名，
Na·sinu nai — en·sinu i·ḫarija·sen, we·pen rini·āt·sen ne sinu.
Na-sinu nai — en-sinu i-kharija-sen, we-pen rini-aat-sen ne sinu. ⲛⲁⲥⲓⲛⲟⲩ ⲛⲁⲓ — ⲉⲛⲥⲓⲛⲟⲩ ⲓϧⲁⲣⲓϫⲁⲥⲉⲛ, ⲩⲉⲡⲉⲛ ⲣⲓⲛⲓⲁ̄ⲧⲥⲉⲛ ⲛⲉ ⲥⲓⲛⲟⲩ. نَسِنُ نَاِ — ئێنسِنُ اِخَرِجَسێن، وێپێن رِنِآتسێن نێ سِنُ.

These two — together-as-pair they emerge, but their names are two.

en·sinu 'in-two, as a pair' — the together-adverbs (R-069): en·wa as-one, en·sinu as-a-pair; 同出 restored to a true 'together' instead of the earlier dissolve. ERRATUM R-071: riniāt·to (3SG 'its names') corrected to riniāt·sen — the two own their names in the plural, as the verb i·ḫarija·sen already conjugated. R-073: ne added (the plural identificational copula's first attestation — the copula doctrine: pe/ne obligatory for nominal equations in the sacred register); Na·sinu nai stands RATIFIED as the solemn postposed demonstrative — Middle Egyptian zj pn syntax surfacing in the register that reaches for antiquity (preposed pen remains the default everywhere)

08同謂之玄。
En·wa i·ranwia·sen: ar·ribesi.
En-wa i-ranwia-sen: ar-ribesi. ⲉⲛⲩⲁ ⲓⲣⲁⲛⲩⲓⲁⲥⲉⲛ: ⲁⲣ̇ⲓⲃⲉⲥⲓ. ئێنوَ اِرَنوِاَسێن: الرِّبێسِ.

Named as one: THE primordial Dark.

en·wa 'in-one, as one' for 同謂 — named-as-one; ranwia, the passive of the ren-root, arrives free from the engine (R-025 -wi-)

09玄之又玄，衆妙之門。
Ar·ribesi en·ribesī — pe na·seba na·pa·biṭeniāt·i.
Ar-ribesi en-ribesii — pe na-seba na-pa-biteniaat-i. ⲁⲣ̇ⲓⲃⲉⲥⲓ ⲉⲛⲣⲓⲃⲉⲥⲓ̄ — ⲡⲉ ⲛⲁⲥⲉⲃⲁ ⲛⲁⲡⲁⲃⲓⲧ̣ⲉⲛⲓⲁ̄ⲧⲓ. الرِّبێسِ ئێنرِبێسي — پێ نَسێبَ نَپَبِطێنِآتِ.

Dark within primordial dark — the gate of all mysteries.

seba — star, gate, and teaching in one root (S-B-ʾ): the Tao closes on the door the education ladder climbs; biṭeniāt pluralizes by R-060's own law. R-073: pe added — dark-within-dark IS the gate: a nominal equation, and the copula doctrine makes the equals sign obligatory in the sacred register
