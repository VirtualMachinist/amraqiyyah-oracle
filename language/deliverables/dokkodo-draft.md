# Na·Hina Mišeyi Waḥīd·i — the Dokkōdō
## DRAFT FOR RATIFICATION — nothing here is sealed until Ali rules (Canon translation law, R-068)

**Source:** Miyamoto Musashi, 獨行道, autograph of Shōhō 2/5/12 (1645) — Kumamoto Prefectural Museum of Art; kana-normalized text via ja.wikisource. Dedicated to Terao Magonojō a week before Musashi's death, alongside the Go Rin No Sho. Tier II-F — The Bushido Foundation (Futuwwa).

**Register decision (steward, for veto):** the Japanese alternates three shapes — 心なし "no heart of X", 事なし "no doing of X", 〜ず "does/do not". The draft mirrors this with two lawful formulas instead of flattening to one: **existential renunciation** *la wi (ib) X·i* for the なし lines (the heart-idiom carrying 心 natively, R-038), and the **prohibitive** *la + imperative* for the ず lines (R-014, exactly lā an-nāhiya). Musashi's first-person vow becomes the precept second person — わが/我 rendered ·ki/·kī where the source has it.

**Sixteen roots coined** (lexicon 273; all sources verified, none fabricated): W-Ḥ-D alone/one (~Al-Wāḥid, pairing #14) · N-ʿ-M pleasure · W-K-L rely (~Al-Wakīl, #15) · N-Ḏ-S lowly (Eg. nḏs, Faulkner 145) · ʿ-M-Q deep · D-N-Y dunyā · Ḥ-S-D envy (Q 113) · ʾ-Ḫ-R other/last (~Al-Āḫir, #16 — Q 57:3 now three-quarters native across Tao + Dokkodo) · F-R-Q part (Q 75:28) · Ḥ-Q-D grudge · Š-K-W complain (Q 12:86) · Ṭ-Y-R omen-superstition (the ḥadīṯ's lā ṭiyara) · Ḫ-P-Š sword (Eg. ḫpš, the khopesh) · M-W-T death (Q 3:185) · Q-D-M old · H-J-R forsake (the hijra-root).

### The twenty-one precepts

**1.** 一 世々の道をそむく事なし
**La ʿadiwa hinā na·pa·zamanāt·i.** — *Oppose not the road of the ages.*
世々 age-upon-age → zamanāt; そむく → ʿadiwa, the enmity-root.

**2.** 一 身にたのしみをたくまず
**La ṭaliba niʿem·ya ra na·jisim·i.** — *Seek no pleasure for the body.*
niʿemi (N-ʿ-M new); たくむ "scheme after" → ṭaliba, the ṭālib's verb turned renunciant.

**3.** 一 よろずに依怙の心なし
**En na·pa·šeyāt·i, la wi ib wikeli·i.** — *In the myriad things, there is no heart of leaning.*
よろず = na·pa·šeyāt, the Tao's own 萬物; 依怙 → wikeli (W-K-L, tawakkul): the trust has one Trustee.

**4.** 一 身をあさく思ひ世をふかく思ふ
**Ḥasiba na·nefes·a naḏīs, ma ḥasiba na·dunyā ʿamīq.** — *Reckon the self shallow, and reckon the world deep.*
naḏīs (Eg. nḏs) one sound from nefes — the lowly and the lower soul are kin; dunyā, ʿamīq new.

**5.** 一 一生の間よくしん思はず
**En na·ʿineki jamīʿ·i, la fakira šiweq·ya.** — *In the whole of life, think no craving-thought.*
šiweqi — the same word that carried the Tao's 欲: one desire across both texts.

**6.** 一 我事におゐて後悔をせず
**Bawt kiyiāt·kī, la ʾasifa.** — *Over your own deeds, have no regret.*
ʾ-S-F (R-049) was waiting for this line.

**7.** 一 善悪に他をねたむ心なし
**En harāy aw kapīl·i, la wi ib et·ḥasida na·ʾeḫar·wa.** — *In good or in ill, no heart that envies the other.*
ḥasida — Q 113:5's envier; ʾeḫaru/ʾaḫīr — Al-Āḫir joins the Tao's Zāhir~Bāṭin pair from the same verse (Q 57:3).

**8.** 一 いづれの道にもわかれをかなしまず
**En na·pa·hināt jamīʿ·i, la ḥazina fireq·ya.** — *On all the roads, grieve not the parting.*
fireqi (F-R-Q, firāq — Q 75:28 "it is the parting").

**9.** 一 自他共にうらみかこつ心なし
**Ra nefes ma ʾeḫar·wi en·wa, la wi ib ḥiqedi·i la šikewi·i.** — *Toward self and other as one, no heart of grudge nor complaint.*
共に → en·wa (R-069) in its second Canon text; šikewi — Yaʿqūb's complaint, made only to Allah.

**10.** 一 れんぼの道思ひよるこゝろなし
**La wi ib et·mašiya ben hina miri·i.** — *No heart that walks toward the road of love-longing.*
mrj unified love and desire — in Egyptian as in Amraqiyyah.

**11.** 一 物毎にすきこのむ事なし
**En šey ma šey·i, la wi miri la kirehi.** — *In thing after thing, no liking nor loathing.*
The preference-pair as love-nor-hate: to prefer nothing is to hold neither.

**12.** 一 私宅におゐてのぞむ心なし
**Bawt hawt·i, la wi ib et·mara.** — *Concerning a dwelling, no heart that desires.*
Musashi gave his possessions away at Reigandō in the week these lines were written.

**13.** 一 身ひとつに美食をこのまず
**Ra jisim waḥīd·i, la mara wonimon jamīl·a.** — *For the single body, crave no fine food.*
waḥīd — the alone-word the title walks on; 美食 calqued: beautiful-food.

**14.** 一 末々代物なる古き道具を所持せず
**La wi ʿind·ki sakimet qadīm ṯamīn.** — *Keep no old and treasured implement.*
ʿind-possession negated existentially: "let there not be, at you…"; a tool is a power-thing (S-K-M).

**15.** 一 わが身にいたり物いみする事なし
**Ra nefes·kī, la ʿabida ṭiyer·ya.** — *For your own self, serve no omen.*
物忌み → ṭiyeri (the ḥadīṯ's lā ṭiyara); ʿabida makes it theological: a superstition served is a god taken.

**16.** 一 兵具は各別よの道具たしなまず
**Na·ḫepeš waḥīd — la mara sakimet ʾaḫīr·a.** — *The sword stands alone — crave no other implement.*
ḫepeš — the khopesh: the sword-saint's 兵具 lands on Kemet's own blade.

**17.** 一 道におゐてハ死をいとはず思ふ
**En al·hinay, fakira na·mawt·a bilā kirehī.** — *On THE Way, think on death without loathing.*
mawt — the mortality field opens; with nefes and ḏawiqa standing, Q 3:185 can now be spoken. al·hina — the sacred article, as the Tao's Way wears it.

**18.** 一 老身に財宝所領もちゆる心なし
**Ra na·simesī, la wi ib et·jamiʿa fulus·a ma yakāt·a.** — *For old age, no heart that gathers money and lands.*
simesi — the abstract the elder-root held in reserve.

**19.** 一 佛神は貴し佛神をたのまず
**Na·pa·ʾilahāt karīm — we·pen la wakila ʿala na·pa·ʾilahāt·i.** — *The gods are noble — yet lean not upon the gods.*
Shahada-shaped: honor what is noble, entrust nothing to it — reliance belongs to Al-Wakīl alone. **Ali steers:** alternative na·pa·qadīsāt "the holy ones" if ʾilahāt reads too strong.

**20.** 一 身を捨ても名利はすてず
**Hajira na·jisim·a — we·pen la hajira na·rin·ya.** — *Forsake the body — yet forsake not the name.*
The hijra-root meets the REN: "to speak a name is to keep a soul alive." Musashi's most Egyptian line. **Ali steers:** kiremi (honor) stands as alternative for 名利.

**21.** 一 常に兵法の道をはなれず
**La·wayen la fariqa min al·hina ḫepeš·i.** — *Never part from THE road of the sword.*
F-R-Q returns from precept 8; negative concord seals the vow. Alternate reading honoring the Tao's 常=dahr: *En dahr, la fariqa…* "In the aeon, part not."

### Questions reserved for Ali (values, the sacred — R-058)

3. **Precept 17/21:** the sacred article al· on Musashi's 道 — ratify or demote to na·?
